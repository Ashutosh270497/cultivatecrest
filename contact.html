Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Click on any product to view details, then click "Buy on Amazon" to complete your purchase securely through Amazon.

What are your delivery times?

Delivery times depend on Amazon's shipping. Typically, Prime members receive products within 1-2 days.

Are your products organic?

We offer a range of both organic and premium conventional products. Check individual product descriptions for details.

What is your return policy?

Returns are handled through Amazon's standard return policy. You can return products within 30 days of delivery.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we serve customers in India through Amazon. For international orders, please contact us directly.

How can I track my order?

After purchasing through Amazon, you'll receive tracking information directly from Amazon in your email.
